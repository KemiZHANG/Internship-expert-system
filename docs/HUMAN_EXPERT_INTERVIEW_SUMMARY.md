## How This Was Converted Into the Knowledge Base

- Directly supported expert points were mapped to facts and rules where the interview explicitly mentioned the readiness factor.
- Indirectly supported points were kept as initial rules that align with the interview and should be discussed in the final report if the expert provided additional comments.
- Unsupported thresholds, such as applying to at least 5 companies, are still marked as needing expert validation.
- KE metadata from `01_Facts_List.csv` and `02_Rule_Base.csv` has been added to `data/facts.json` and `data/rules.json` using fields such as `evidence_status`, `expert_alignment_status`, and `knowledge_engineering_note`.
